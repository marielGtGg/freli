About

Since I moved to Freli, I'm loving it. And I want you to come enjoy it with me. So, I show you my favourites, the little pearls that make Freli the coolest village in Quebec… nothing less!

Freli, it's people who love life and who love the land and what it offers us. There are small farms here and there, a farmer's market on Saturdays, a small primary school perched on a hill between the trees. It's an impromptu mini-putt party at the local restaurant, a Martha Wainwright show in a café the size of my hand, it's a weekday picnic by the Brochets River. Freli is a bit of all that, and a lot of possibilities, too.

Come by, you won't regret it.
